Academic Programs

Our school offers a wide range of academic programs designed to nurture students’ talents, build strong foundations, and prepare them for future success. From early childhood education to upper primary and secondary, we ensure each learner receives quality instruction in every subject.

Programs

Our Classes

Nursery & Pre-Primary

Foundational learning in literacy, numeracy, play-based activities, and social skills to prepare young learners for formal schooling.

Lower Primary (Grade 1–3)

Core subjects include English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, Environmental Activities, Hygiene, and Creative Arts.

Upper Primary (Grade 4–6)

Subjects include Science, Social Studies, Agriculture, English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, CRE/IRE, and Creative Arts.

Junior Secondary (Grade 7–9)

A blend of core subjects and pre-career studies such as ICT, Business Studies, Agriculture, and Technical Subjects.

Senior Secondary (Grade 10–12)

Specialization pathways in Sciences, Arts & Humanities, and Technical/Vocational Studies to prepare learners for careers and higher education.
