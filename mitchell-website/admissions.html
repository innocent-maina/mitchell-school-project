Admissions

At Young Stars Elementary School, we welcome learners from diverse backgrounds into a nurturing and supportive environment. Our admissions process is simple and transparent, designed to make your child’s transition smooth and enjoyable.

  1. Home
  2. Admissions

Admission Requirements

  • Baby Class (Nursery): 3 – 4 years old
  • Pre-Primary 1 (PP1): 4 – 5 years old
  • Pre-Primary 2 (PP2): 5 – 6 years old
  • Grade 1 – 3: 6 – 9 years old
  • Transfer Students: Must provide a transfer letter, recent report card, and birth certificate.

How to Apply

  1. Visit our school office or download the admission form online.
  2. Submit a completed form with your child’s birth certificate and passport photos.
  3. Schedule an interview/assessment with our admissions team.
  4. Pay the registration fee to secure your child’s place.
  5. Receive an official admission letter and joining instructions.
