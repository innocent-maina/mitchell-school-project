About Us

At Young Stars Elementary School, we believe every child is a shining star. We provide a nurturing environment where curiosity, creativity, and confidence grow hand in hand. Guided by the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC), we focus on building strong values, foundational skills, and a love for learning.

Our Mission & Vision

To nurture young minds with knowledge, skills, and values that prepare them for a bright future while inspiring confidence and responsibility.

  • Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) with emphasis on creativity and exploration.
  • Holistic growth through academics, co-curricular activities, and life skills.
  • Safe, inclusive, and nurturing environment where every child feels valued.
