Academic Programs

Our school provides a nurturing environment where learners grow through structured classes from Baby Care to Grade 3. Each stage is carefully designed to build strong foundations and prepare pupils for the next academic level.

Programs

Our Classes

Baby Care

A safe and nurturing environment for infants with focus on care, play, and early stimulation.

Play Group

Introduction to social interaction, creativity, and basic motor skills through play-based learning.

Pre-Primary 1

Focus on language, numeracy, and exploration to build a strong academic foundation.

Pre-Primary 2

Preparation for Grade 1 with structured literacy, numeracy, and creative activities.

Grade 1

Formal learning begins with English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, and Environmental Activities.

Grade 2

Enhancing literacy, numeracy, and life skills through engaging classroom and outdoor activities.

Grade 3

Consolidating learning with added emphasis on science, arts, and environmental awareness.
